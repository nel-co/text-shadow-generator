The Green Table

The Green Table is a food and lifestyle blog primarily targeted to women between 25 and 60 years old. The blog content focuses on using organic, sustainable, and local ingredients sourced from farms and CSA sites. The goal while creating the site was to make it "modern but comfortable and homey." Also to focus the eye's attention to the photography but also present the text in an easy to read way.
